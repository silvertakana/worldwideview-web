# Data Accuracy Disclaimer

**Last Updated:** May 8, 2026

WorldWideView is a powerful geospatial visualization engine. However, users must understand the limitations of the data presented on the 3D globe.

## 1. Nature of the Data
The data displayed within WorldWideView (including aircraft positions, maritime vessel AIS data, satellite telemetry, conflict events, and environmental tracking) is aggregated from public APIs, open-source feeds, and community-seeded plugins. 

**WorldWideView is a visualizer, not a data originator.**

All data is provided strictly on an **"AS IS"** and **"AS AVAILABLE"** basis.
- Flight paths, vessel positions, and event markers may be delayed by several minutes or hours.
- Data feeds may drop, become corrupted, or display inaccurate coordinates due to upstream API failures, sensor ghosting, or spoofing.
- Community-developed plugins may contain bugs or misinterpret the data they fetch.

We make no representations or warranties, express or implied, regarding the accuracy, timeliness, completeness, or reliability of any data rendered on the platform.
